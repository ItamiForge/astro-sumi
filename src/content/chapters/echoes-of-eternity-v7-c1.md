# Chapter 1: Splitting the Atom

The mushroom cloud rose over the desert, a pillar of fire and death that dwarfed anything humanity had created before. Dr. Kael Thornton watched through darkened glass and felt a chill that had nothing to do with the desert night.

He had seen destruction before. In dreams, in memories that weren't his own. But nothing like this.

Humanity had learned to split the atom. And in doing so, they had created a darkness of their own making.
