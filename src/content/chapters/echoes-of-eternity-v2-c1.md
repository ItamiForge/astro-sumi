# Chapter 1: Forged in Iron

The Roman Empire stretched across the known world, its legions marching under eagles of bronze and iron. In a small garrison town on the frontier, a young centurion named Marcus woke from a dream of bronze swords and shadow creatures.

He shouldn't remember. The cycle was broken. The five heroes had sacrificed themselves to end it forever.

So why did he remember?
