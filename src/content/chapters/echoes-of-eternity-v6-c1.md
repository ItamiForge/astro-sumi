# Chapter 1: Current of Change

The lights of New York City blazed against the night sky, powered by the miracle of electricity. Kael stood on the Brooklyn Bridge and watched the city that never slept, feeling the hum of power in the air.

It reminded him of magic. Of a time when power came from within, not from wires and generators.
