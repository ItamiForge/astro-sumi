# Template Author

This is an example author profile that shows how creative writers can present themselves using Astro Sumi.

## About the Author

Template Author (pen name: T.A. Example) is a fictional author created to demonstrate the capabilities of this novel writing template. This profile showcases how authors can present their writing background, genres, and social media presence.

## Writing Background

This author specializes in fantasy, science fiction, and mystery genres, demonstrating how the template can accommodate various types of creative writing. The author schema supports multiple social media platforms and funding sources commonly used by independent writers.

## Template Usage

When using this template for your own writing project:

1. Replace all the information in the frontmatter with your own details
2. Update the bio and content sections with your actual writing background
3. Add or remove social media links as needed
4. Replace the avatar image with your own author photo
